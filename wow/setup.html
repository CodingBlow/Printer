Quick Troubleshooting Guide

1

Clear the Print Queue

Often, a stuck document prevents communication. Go to Settings > Devices > Printers. Open your print queue and select 'Cancel All Documents'.

2

The 60-Second Power Cycle

Unplug your printer and your Wi-Fi router. Wait 60 seconds. Plug the router in first, wait for internet access, then plug in the printer.

3

Driver Verification

Ensure you aren't using the "Generic" driver provided by the OS. Download the full software suite from the official manufacturer portal for full feature support.
