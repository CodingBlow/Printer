About Googlai

Who We Are

Building the Logic for a Better Future

Founded in 2018, Googlai started with a simple idea: Intelligence is evenly distributed, but opportunity is not. We are a registered non-profit organization focused on democratizing education through technology.

Our approach is scientific and logic-driven. We don't just donate books; we build ecosystems. From solar-powered computer labs to teacher training programs, every initiative is measured for impact and sustainability.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

At Googlai, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data in accordance with Indian and International laws.

1. Information Collection
