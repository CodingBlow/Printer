Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Quick Setup Assist ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out forms on our website (like the callback request form) or communicate with us.

  • Contact Data: Your name and phone number.
  • Device Data: Your printer model and a description of the issue you are experiencing (e.g., "WiFi Connection Error").
  • Technical Data: Your IP address, browser type, and operating system (collected automatically for secure remote connection purposes).

How We Use Your Information (Crucial for Google Safety)

We use the collected information for the following specific purposes:

  • To contact you directly regarding your setup assistance request.
  • To schedule a technical callback or remote configuration session.
  • To diagnose and resolve the connection issues with your device.
  • To improve our independent setup services.

We DO NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information we collect from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
